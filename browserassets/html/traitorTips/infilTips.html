You are a Syndicate infiltrator!

1. Your goal is to complete the objectives assigned to you.

2. Each infiltrator starts with a requisition token.
Insert it in to a weapons vendor and select a loadout and utility option from the list.
It is often worthwhile to plan your loadout purchases with your fellow infiltrators!

3. Teamplay is key! Discuss the mission with your comrades and come up with a solid plan before anybody heads out. Use your headset to stay in touch:

say :h I'm about to get the Head of Security's beret.

4. To exit the Syndicate Battlecruiser Cairngorm, take a pod from the podbay and open a wormhole leading to the station. Be aware that Pods cannot warp back to the Cairngorm.

5. For more information, please consult the wiki.
